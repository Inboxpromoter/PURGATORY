Parking Updates
This season we have updated our parking protocols. Starting this Saturday, December 18th, during weekends and holidays, the Main Village Lot will be free for vehicles with four or more passengers and Durango Mountain Club (DMC) members. Otherwise, Village Lot parking is available for $20. For free parking, we recommend parking in the Columbine and Gelande Lots. Click here for more information on the new parking protocols.
New Terrain Alert
Thanks to 10 inches of snow this week and hard work from our mountain ops team, we were able to open Styx, Lower Hades, Pandemonium and Gelande. With more snow helping us to open new terrain, make sure to keep your eye on the snow report for the most up-to-date information on weather, lift status and onmountain conditions.
